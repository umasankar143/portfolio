Solar Tracker System Project

  • Developed a solar tracking system to enhance panel efficiency.
  • Used LDR sensors to detect sun's position in real time.
  • Implemented Arduino for processing sensor input and control logic.
  • Integrated servo motors to adjust the panel’s angle dynamically.
  • Increased energy capture compared to static panel setups.
  • Designed with a focus on low power usage and cost-effectiveness.
  • Supports sustainable energy through optimized solar harvesting.
